LiCoO2

Posted on 2022-09-19 Edited on 2022-09-23 In Anode material

Physical performance :

​ The commonly used lithium cobalt oxide is a layered structure with relatively stable structure. During charging and discharging, lithium ions can undergo reversible descalation/embedding reactions from the plane in which they are located.

Preparation method :

​ The solid phase reaction is generally carried out at high temperature. Lithium carbonate and cobalt oxide were mixed, and adhesives were added for granulation before reaction. In order to overcome the shortcomings of solid phase reaction ,sol-gel method, spray decomposition method, sedimentation method and spray drying method can be used. Lithium cobalt oxide is prepared by sol-gel method. Generally, cobalt salt is dissolved first, and then the PH is gradually adjusted by LiOH and ammonia to form gel.
